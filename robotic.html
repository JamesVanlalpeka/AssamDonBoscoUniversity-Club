ROBOTIS'S CLUB

The Robotics Club of Assam Don Bosco University is a student-led organization that focuses on promoting education about latest trends of robotics. The club aims to equip its members with the necessary skills and knowledge to know about the prior knowledge of robotics or working principle of robots etc...

In ADBU robotics club, students typically have access to resources such as 3D printers, microcontrollers, sensors, and other tools that they can use to build and program robots. They can also take part in competitions and events that challenge them to design and build robots for specific tasks or challenges.
